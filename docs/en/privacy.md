# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 15, 2026

UNO Robotics ("we", "our", or "us") provides this app to allow authorized users to access and monitor IoT data and related services.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

## 1. Information We Process

The app may process the following information:

- **Access credentials entered by authorized users**
- **Configuration data required to connect to services**
- **ThingSpeak channel access information**, including access to public and private channels
- **Operational data** required for the app to retrieve, display, and manage IoT information

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information above only for the operation of the app, including:

- authenticating authorized access
- securely storing access information
- connecting to public and private ThingSpeak channels
- displaying channel data and related app content
- maintaining app functionality and service reliability

## 3. Storage and Security

Credentials and related access information entered in the app are stored using Firebase in encrypted form.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect stored information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

## 4. Third-Party Services

This app uses third-party services that may process information as necessary for their functionality, including:

- **Firebase** – for secure backend storage and related application functionality
- **ThingSpeak** – for retrieving and interacting with IoT channel data

Use of these services is also subject to their own terms and privacy practices.

## 5. Data Sharing

UNO Robotics does not sell personal data.

We do not use the information processed by the app for third-party advertising.

Information is only processed and shared as needed to operate the app and its connected services.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain stored credentials and related access information only for as long as necessary to provide app functionality, maintain security, support authorized access, and comply with legal or operational requirements.

## 7. User Rights and Deletion Requests

Authorized users may request deletion of stored credentials or related access information by contacting us.

## 8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request deletion of stored data, contact:

**UNO Robotics**  
**Email:** info@uno-robotics.com

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
